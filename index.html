FAQ

Questions Answered.

How long does a logo design take? +
Typically 2–4 business days. We deliver 3 unique concepts and include up to 3 revisions. All final files delivered in AI, PNG and PDF formats.
Do you work with clients worldwide? +
Yes — 100% remote, Worldwide. All deliverables are sent digitally via WhatsApp, email and Google Drive. No location limits whatsoever.
What is GEO and why does my business need it? +
GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) gets your brand cited as an answer in ChatGPT, Google Gemini and Perplexity. As AI search replaces traditional search, GEO is the new SEO.
What is included in the Starter Brand Identity package? +
Logo design with 3 unique concepts, brand color palette, business card design, social media kit and all source files — everything to launch a professional brand. WhatsApp us to discuss your project.
How do I pay? What are the payment terms? +
We accept UPI, bank transfer and Google Pay. Payment is 50% advance and 50% on delivery. Full receipt issued for every payment. WhatsApp us to discuss your project.
Can I start small and upgrade later? +
Absolutely. Start with Starter, upgrade to Growth or Pro when ready. We credit your previous payment and ensure a smooth transition at every stage.
